Donate to Support Streator Masonic Lodge Initiatives

Your contribution makes a direct impact on the community. Choose a donation amount and help fund meaningful programs that uplift, educate, and support those in need. Choose Your Donation Amount:

Where Your Donations Go:

Each contribution helps fund these key initiatives and more:

  • Woodland and Streator Scholarship Fund: Empowering students through financial aid for higher education. Giving four $1,000 dollar Scholarships per year
  • Woodland High School Basketball Team: For the last 4 years, Streator Masonic Lodges has been helping the team pay for the huddle system a total of approximately $5,000 as of 2025
  • Local Club Donation: $1,000 donation towards all their activies to help the Streator community; like Key Club, FFA, ect... (Each year one club is selected)
  • Books for Bikes Program: Rewarding young readers with bicycles, fostering education and activity. Kimes, Woodland, and Centennial Elementary Schools
  • Coat Drive: Providing warmth to kids who need it the most during cold months.
  • Kids Fun Nights: Social events that create joy and engagement for local youth.
  • Christmas at ARC Nursing Home Get gifts for these that might not have anyone, showing that someone still cares
  • Widows and Shut-in Brothers: Supporting those in isolation or financial distress.
  • Streator Scholastic Bowl Team: Encouraging academic excellence and teamwork.
  • Breakfast with Santa Event: Each year we host a community breakfast with santa, with Free Picture, Breakfast, and presents for the kids
  • Kids Rec Room Renovations: Enhancing recreational spaces for a better community experience.

Why Donate?

Your generosity fuels programs that make a tangible difference in our community. Whether it’s education, support, or enrichment, your gift helps strengthen Streator’s future.
