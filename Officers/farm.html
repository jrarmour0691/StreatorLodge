Mission Statement

The Farm Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is committed to the responsible management and long‑term care of the Lodge’s agricultural property. Our mission is to oversee the annual rental agreement, ensure fair and transparent partnerships with local farmer, and maintain the land in a manner that reflects the values of stewardship, integrity, and community service.

We work to preserve the productivity and health of the farm ground through regular evaluation, proper maintenance, and thoughtful planning. By managing this important asset with diligence and foresight, the Committee helps support the Lodge’s charitable work, financial stability, and ongoing contributions to the Streator community
