Mission Statement

The Ritual Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to preserving the integrity, beauty, and meaning of our Masonic work. Our mission is to provide Brothers with opportunities to learn, practice, and confidently perform the standard work of our degrees, ensuring that our ceremonies are delivered with accuracy, dignity, and respect for tradition.

We offer guidance, structured practice, and mentorship to help members strengthen their ritual proficiency and explore new roles within the Lodge.
