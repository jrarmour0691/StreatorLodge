Mission Statement

The Finance Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to ensuring the responsible and transparent stewardship of the Lodge’s financial resources. Our mission is to support the Lodge’s charitable work, educational programs, and community outreach by managing funds with care, integrity, and long‑term vision.

We work to maintain clear financial practices, provide reliable guidance to Lodge leadership, and uphold the trust placed in us by our members and community partners. Through thoughtful planning and prudent budgeting, we help strengthen the Lodge’s ability to serve others and preserve the values of Freemasonry for future generations.
