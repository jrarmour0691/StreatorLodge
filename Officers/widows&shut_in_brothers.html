Mission Statement

The Widows & Shut‑In Brothers Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to upholding one of Freemasonry’s most cherished obligations: caring for those who can no longer be with us in Lodge. Our mission is to provide meaningful support, companionship, and assistance to the widows of our departed Brothers and to those members who are homebound, hospitalized, or facing challenges that limit their participation.

We strive to maintain regular contact, offer help when needed, and ensure that these valued members of our Masonic family feel remembered, respected, and connected. Through visits, calls, cards, and acts of service, the Committee works to demonstrate the enduring bonds of brotherly love and the Lodge’s commitment to compassion and fellowship.
