Mission Statement

The Academic Bowl Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to encouraging academic excellence, teamwork, and intellectual growth among local students. Our mission is to support and promote the annual Academic Bowl competition, providing an opportunity for young people to challenge themselves, showcase their knowledge, and develop skills that will serve them throughout their lives.
