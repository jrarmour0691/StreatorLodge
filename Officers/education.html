Mission Statement

The Education Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to enriching the Masonic knowledge, personal growth, and fraternal engagement of our members. Our mission is to ensure that every stated meeting includes meaningful educational content that deepens understanding of Masonic history, symbolism, ritual, and values.

We work to provide thoughtful presentations, discussions, and learning opportunities that inspire reflection and strengthen the bonds of brotherhood. By fostering a culture of curiosity and lifelong learning, the Education Committee helps members connect more deeply with the teachings of Freemasonry and apply its principles in their daily lives.
