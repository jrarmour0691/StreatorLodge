Mission Statement

The Membership Retention Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to fostering a welcoming, supportive, and engaging experience for every Brother of the Lodge. Our mission is to strengthen member involvement by promoting fellowship, encouraging participation, and ensuring that all members feel valued, informed, and connected to the life of the Lodge.

We work to identify the needs and interests of our members, support their ongoing Masonic journey, and provide outreach to those who may be facing challenges or drifting away from active participation. Through regular communication, personal engagement, and thoughtful programming, the Committee helps cultivate a sense of belonging and brotherhood that endures.
