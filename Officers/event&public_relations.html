Mission Statement

The Events & Public Relations Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to strengthening the Lodge’s presence within the community through meaningful engagement, clear communication, and well‑organized events. Our mission is to create opportunities that showcase the values of Freemasonry, foster fellowship among members, and build positive relationships with the public.

We plan and coordinate events that support the Lodge’s charitable initiatives, educational programs, and fraternal traditions, ensuring they are welcoming, impactful, and aligned with our core principles. Through thoughtful outreach, transparent communication, and collaboration with community partners, we work to share the story of our Lodge and highlight the good we strive to do in Streator and beyond.

By promoting connection, service, and visibility, the Events & Public Relations Committee helps strengthen the Lodge’s role as a trusted and active contributor to the community.
