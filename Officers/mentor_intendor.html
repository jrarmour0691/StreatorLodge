Mission Statement

The Mentor/Intender Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to guiding new Brothers as they begin their Masonic journey. Our mission is to provide thoughtful instruction, personal support, and meaningful mentorship that helps each candidate understand the teachings, traditions, and responsibilities of Freemasonry.

We work to ensure that every new Brother is paired with a knowledgeable and supportive mentor who can answer questions, explain the degrees, and help them learn the standard work at a comfortable and encouraging pace. Through regular engagement, structured learning, and genuine fellowship, the Committee helps new members feel welcomed, prepared, and confident as they grow within the Craft
