Mission Statement

The Building Committee of Streator Masonic Lodge No. 607 is dedicated to preserving, maintaining, and improving the physical home of our Lodge. Our mission is to ensure that the Lodge building remains safe, welcoming, and functional for members, guests, and the community organizations we support.

We oversee the upkeep of the facility, plan and prioritize repairs, and guide long‑term improvements. Through responsible stewardship, thoughtful planning, we work to create an environment that reflects the values of Freemasonry—stability, care, and a commitment to future generations.
